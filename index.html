Signal-weighted recommended weights.

  • NESTLE / COLG / FCEPL / SHEZ: 8.1% each (floor)

Signal logic

Weights combine three signals: SML alpha (excess return vs CAPM), DDM premium/discount, and inverse volatility tilt. UPFL and RMPL are rewarded for low drawdown and strong DDM undervaluation. NATF earns 13% for outstanding Sharpe (1.38) and alpha generation. The 8.1% floor maintains breadth across all names.

Variance decomposition

Systematic (β-related) risk accounts for approximately 14% of total portfolio variance. The remaining 86% is unsystematic — diversifiable through cross-asset allocation. This confirms the diversification benefit is structural, not incidental.

Conclusion

Low correlation. Strong alpha. Real value.

PSX's Food & Personal Care sector offers exceptional diversification within a single defensive sector. NATF, SHEZ, MFL, and FCEPL each earned 17–50 pp above their CAPM-required return over the window. The signal-weighted portfolio captures this alpha at lower volatility than an equal-weight baseline. Quarterly rebalancing recommended.
